Your Actions
FoldQuit. Lose your bet.
CheckPass. Only if nobody bet.
CallMatch the current bet.
RaiseIncrease the bet.
Blinds: Forced bets that rotate. Small blind = ½ min. Big blind = full min. Keeps the game moving.
Position (Huge Advantage)

Quick Odds to Know
Pocket pair → set on flop~12%
Flush draw → flush by river~35%
Open-ended straight draw~32%
Two overcards → pair by river~24%
Getting dealt AA~0.5%
Flopping a set with a pair~7.5:1

Rule of 2 and 4: Count your “outs.” Multiply by 4 after the flop, or 2 after the turn, for rough % to hit.

10 Rules for Your First Game
1
Fold more. Seriously. 70-80% of hands.
2
Position is everything. Tighter early, looser on the button.
3
Don't chase. Need one specific card? Probably fold.
4
Watch the board. 3 hearts = someone has a flush.
5
Bet with purpose. Value or bluff. Never “just because.”
6
Respect big raises. Tight player shoves? They have it.
7
Manage your stack. No all-in on marginal hands early.
8
Watch the players. Tight? Loose? Aggressive? Adjust.
9
Don't show cards. Fold face down. Keep them guessing.
10
Stay calm. Bad beats happen. Don't tilt.
Tiebreakers (Kickers)
Same hand type? Highest cards within win. Both have pair of Kings? Highest remaining card (“kicker”) wins. All 5 identical ranks = split pot. Suits never break ties.
